# EverythingTracker

A small, opinionated daily tracker built around one question: **how much of your day actually went where you wanted it to go?**

It's a single page app. No account, no server, no sync. Your data never leaves your browser.

Available at: https://tsidia.github.io/EverythingTracker/

---

## Features

- **Daily task list** with minute budgets, drag-to-reorder, and one-click timers.

<img width="1919" height="769" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1f84c3ae-13ec-48a5-b9f4-34b04d5b8589" />

- **Focus timer** per-task, stays running even if you tab away. Minimizes into the nav bar so you can peek at it without losing what you were doing.

<img width="1909" height="884" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/54c421ba-d666-4216-b8ef-81f94d5c459a" />

- **Meditation mode** with a pleasant brown-noise generator and a chime every five minutes.

<img width="1894" height="878" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e10d07dd-11b5-4bb2-905b-43223e224ca6" />
